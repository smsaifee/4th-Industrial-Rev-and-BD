## 5.6 CHALLENGE IN AGRICULTURE

Bangladeshi agricultural field will face some challenges if 4th IR is thoroughly practiced in the society such provocations are

- Reducing agricultural land because of pollution created by advanced mercenaries
- The percentage of farmers will also decline.
- Unskilled populations are also a big challenge in this sector because they cannot cooperate with advanced pieces of machinery.
- Reskilling the existing farmers is another big challenge because they are not ready to operate new machinery; the lack of training is the main reason behind this gap.
