### \***\*4.13\*\*** \***\*IMPACT ON TOURISM AND HOSPITALITY\*\***

**The use** of AI and IoT in the tourism sector is widespread nowadays. The Bangladeshi tourism sector is now evolving rapidly. Modern needs are created new problems and requirements. Customers now want maximum travel experience with minimum costs. On the other hand, hotels and restaurants are engaging in many new investments as the environment changes. Street restaurants are very ordinary nowadays, adopting modern technological supports. Food delivery is now a pervasive culture in Bangladeshi metropolises, a dream in just a decade. Online advertising adds new tourism destinations to the mass people's bucket lists. Some focal AI-based practices in Bangladeshi tourism sectors are

- - - - Online ticket and room booking - Virtual customers experiences - Online advertising - Social media adventure groups and get together - Customized tour plan for every customer - Digital travel agencies - Actual feedback forms the customers - Customer's data analysis and finding out suitable destinations
