### \***\*4.6.4 MEDICAL IMPACT OF ROBOTICS\*\***

Bangladeshi medical care sector is now engaging robotics and Artificial components in the treatment; artificial organ transplanting is gaining popularity. The hospitals in Bangladesh often face enormous pressure in handling the patients; a robot in the reception and robot nurses may be a good alternative in this sector. Moreover, artificial organs like prosthetic legs, hands, and artificial hearts are increasing in the Bangladeshi medical industry.
