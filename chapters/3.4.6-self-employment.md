### 2.3.6 THE FIELD OF SELF-EMPLOYMENT

Fourth industrial revolution allows for new ideas to be explored outside of the box. Our company can get off the ground without having to invest in a farm. Anyone may start their own business with the aid of precocious technological support. In the corporate world, the word "start-ups" is becoming a widespread one. However, the full picture of this episode is far more interesting and startling. To create a common ground for consumers and customers, previous industrial revolutions were unable. Despite this, algorithms are capable of comprehending the expectations of both customers and consumers. A new type of small business entrepreneurship is born out of this mutual understanding. Entrepreneurship in the 21st century is becoming more and more common.

- Small-scale travel and tourism
- The creation of software
- Agroforestry
- Education
- processing of food
- Textile
- Media
- Mechanical devices of a more compact size
- Food center
