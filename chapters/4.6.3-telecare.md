### \***\*4.6.3 TELE CARE\*\***

Remote medical care is already a widespread practice in Bangladesh. To fulfill the following generation criteria, it is a most needed element. The current covid situation opens many paths of telecare. For example, an oxygen saturation meter played a considerable role in treating covid 19. Other devices like smart watches, digital pressure machines, and other advanced equipment are now available in the Bangladeshi market. These products not only monitor the patients' but also reduce hospital admission. Digital pathological labs use advanced IoT technologies to predict almost correctly.
