### 4.7.2 WASTE MANAGEMENT

Bangladeshi waste management system is not up to the mark, and IoT in this sector has not been introduced yet. However, there is a possibility for introducing a new green waste management system. Implementing digital waste bins to send notifications to the nearest waste management unit is an excellent example of a city-based waste management system. other waste management ideas are -

- - - - Self-driven waste management vehicles - IoT monitoring of waste bins - Advanced waste plants and machinery can be alarming at the right time.
