### 2.3.3 THE HOTEL AND TOURISM INDUSTRIES

Traveling is no longer a common occurrence in our lives. When it comes to our travels, we've gotten so granular. It's now possible for everyone to have a good time because to tourism. Everything in the tourism industry revolves around making customers happy and providing them with an enjoyable experience. There has been a rise in the application of machine learning in the tourist business in order to attract more clients. It's common for AI-based technologies to manage anything from mobile applications to websites to internet advertising. In order to expand their company, hotels and restaurants are increasingly using sophisticated connectivity metrics. Nowadays, many people employ home services.
