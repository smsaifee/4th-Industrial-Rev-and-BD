## 5.16 PSYCHOLOGICAL BARRIERS OR THE GENERATION GAP

It is the human mind that created the advanced world. Our imagination brought us to hare in the zenith of an advanced civilization. Hence, we have some human limits bound to certain norms and emotions. Our collective superiority also had a history of collective failures, blood shades, and many more. The upcoming revolution impacts our mundane lives and forces us to take steps that we do not deserve. Nevertheless, the fate of civilization brings us to a position of complete madness and unprecedented developments. The human mind is an evolving organ that is not ready to adopt sudden changes. However, we need changes to continue our journey to complete advancement. The main psychological barriers in this process are accepting the new changes and competing with the new generations. The world is not the same as we observed fifteen years ago. Some common psychological challenges that we are about to face are

- Facing the new generation peoples
- The lacking's of knowledge in the current generation
- New practices in the society
- Cultural diversity and changes in the practice
