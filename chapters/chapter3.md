# Chapter3

The Fourth Industrial Revolution is about how the lines between the real, digital, and biological worlds are becoming less clear. It is a mix of advances in artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, the Internet of Things (IoT), 3D printing, genetic engineering, quantum computing, and other technologies. It is the force behind a lot of products and services that are becoming more and more important to modern life. Because of this perfect storm of technologies, the Fourth Industrial Revolution is paving the way for big changes in how we live and is causing big problems in almost every sector. All of this is happening at a speed that has never been seen before. This chapter is mostly about the things you need to look at and study to understand how deep 4IR goes. The effects of 4IR on the whole world are huge and much more important than we all think. So, it is technically impossible to find all of the possible effects of 4IR in the modern world. But the chapter tried to show the different aspects and effects of 4IR
