### \***\*4.7.5 SMART BUILDING\*\***

This idea of an intelligent building is quite a new phenomenon for Bangladeshi culture. Using the building facilities entirely and reducing the dissipation is the rudimentary aim of an intelligent building. Especially the city areas and government projects are suitable for this. To improve this sector, first, we have to categorize the sectors. The metropolis of Bangladesh is growing very fast, but there is a lack of planning in the cityscape. Every year the big cities of Bangladesh face some common problems like -

- - - - Drainage problem - Lack of transportation - Unplanned roads, buildings - Waste management of buildings - Lack of proper building codes

The use of AI has already been implemented in various buildings. Some fourth industrial use in the buildings

- - - - Proper energy flow - Automatic maintenance - Building security - Interior designs by algorithms to maximize the use of space.
