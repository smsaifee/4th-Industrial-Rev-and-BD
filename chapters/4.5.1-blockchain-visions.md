### 4.5.1 VISIONS OF BLOCKCHAIN IN BANGLADESHI CONTEXT

- Develop resilient infrastructure
- e-Governance
- Promote innovation
- Facilitate fair competition
- Reduce intermediary
- Ensure accountability and transparency
- Increase trust
- Guarantee information security and privacy
- Involve all stakeholders
