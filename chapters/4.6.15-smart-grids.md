### \***\*4.7.6\*\*** \***\*SMART GRIDS\*\***

A smart grid is an electricity network based on digital technology that supplies consumers via two-way digital communication. This system allows for monitoring, analysis, control, and communication within the supply chain to help improve efficiency, reduce energy consumption and cost, and maximize the transparency and reliability of the energy supply chain. The smart grid was introduced to overcome the weaknesses of conventional electrical grids by using smart net meters. Bangladeshi authority already determined to modify innovative card system in gas, electricity and water supply. Already metropolitan areas are operating these facilities. This technology ensures the correct use of every power.
