## 3.2 MAINSTREAM MANUFACTURING INDUSTRIES

The industrial revolutions are changing the technical side of production and creating many new industries. Before we go through the global impacts of the 4th IR, we have to identify the sectors where the industrial revolution took place. The first important sector is manufacturing and customer's behaviors. The primary aim of any industrialization process is to enhance the production line. The industries are now using advanced technological equipment and software to develop low-cost and high production of any goods. Economists have been predicted that within 50 years, the entire manufacturing industry will face an immense number of technical changes that may include many insecure jobs. The use of complete automation may eradicate the benefit of humans in the manufacturing line<sup>[\[14\]](#footnote-14)</sup>.
