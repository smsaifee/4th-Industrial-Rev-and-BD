### \***\*4.10 ADMINISTRATIVE\*\***

The archaic method of using analog registration is almost eradicated from every official sector. Digital biometric recognition systems, prediction of the legal outcome, research, government data analysis, financial recommendation, and digital identification are some commonly used AI technologies. On the other hand, almost every governmental office has its websites and social media pages. The current covid situation shows us the power of connectivity where many vital activities are practiced online. Bangladeshi organizational culture is approaching a new dimension of connectivity by using the 4th IR. Some practices are

- - - - Video interviews - Online official meetings - Sharing the information via social media - Improve the employee experience - Connecting the employee is so that they can share their ideas - Data-driven management system - Meticulous managing system
