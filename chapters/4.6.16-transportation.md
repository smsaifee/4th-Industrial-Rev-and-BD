### \***\*4.8\*\*** \***\*IMPACT ON TRANSPORTATION\*\***

The transportation system in Bangladesh is slowly upgrading its fields. Hance the developments is not much speedy. However, the recent introduction of ride-sharing platforms is quite popular in metropolitan areas. A platform like E-currier, Redx, food panda,'PATHAO' and Uber run their business without investing in any cars. Just a polished algorithm is behind all these modern systems. Another is traffic control, where IoT can enhance the situation. The entire traffic system has centralized connections and interconnected facilities then the machine could automatically perform. This automation in traffic needs the technology of IoT. Some other opportunities for Bangladeshi perspectives are

- - - - Digital AI-based tickets - Advanced machine learning techniques for potential future risks - Use of IoT technologies to reduce pollution - Monitoring of vehicles - Using sensors in the vehicles to check the future risks - Maintenance of the vehicles by advanced robotics - Manufacturing vehicle industry and robotics
