## THE OBJECTIVE OF THE STUDY

The fourth industrial revolution is a modern phenomenon for all of us, and it needs new ideas and research. The primary aim of this writing is to relate this massive change with the Bangladeshi perspective. The rudimentary objectives are -

- To know about the fourth industrial revolution and the importance of this revolution.
- To understand the global perspective of 4IR
- To find out the current position and impact of 4IR in Bangladesh.
- To find the challenges and suggest possible recommendations to the implication of 4IR in Bangladesh.
