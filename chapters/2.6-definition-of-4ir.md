## 2.6 DEFINITION OF THE FOURTH INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION

To summarise the industrial revolution, we must focus on some core points of the 4rh industrial revolution. The fourth industrial revolution is the connection between our dream and reality. The focal points of the fourth industrial revolution are the interconnection between machines and humans, the technical advancement of humans and apparatus, the automation of factories and human jobs, and massive production with the help of simple but effective advanced technologies. The use of the process is technical advancement and simplifying that machinery. The fourth industrial revolution produced better production of goods and enhanced human civilization's education, politics, and social condition. The connection between machines and humans depends on how we see these changes. 4IR does not stand for a particular society or region; it is operated and directly impacts every nation's culture. We do not have any choice to avoid the consequences<sup>[\[9\]](#footnote-9)</sup>.

The fourth industrial revolution is a process of eradicating the boundaries between the physical and digital world. By using unprecedented methods and technologies, the connectivity and coexistence of machines and humans are achieved. The changes are so vivid that we cannot ignore the impacts on our daily life.
